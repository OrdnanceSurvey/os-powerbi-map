Welcome to the Ordnance Survey PowerBI Visual!

Basemaps

This visual uses the OS maps API to provide backdrop mapping for your data. Our open data includes Roads, Outdoor or Light styles. To use premium data in this product, such as the Leisure style and extra zoom levels, you will need an API key. Click here to get one. Alternatively opendata will be used.

To create a map, add some data! You can plot points on the map by dragging and dropping from your data into the fields provided in the Visualizations pane. You can use a UPRN or Postcode field which automatically geocodes your inputs. The X and Y coordinate fields can be used with Longitude and Latitude or Easting and Northing. We also support WKT and GeoJSON geometries.
